About CIT

Cognitive Intelligence test is the newest member in the chapter's list of events. It is an online event where sets of questions are designed similar to that of a screening process in company.CIT not only provides an idea about the level of competition but also gives a chance to rectify oneself. The test comprises of basic mathematical aptitude, verbal and logical reasoning and data interpretation. This is one of the finest opportunities to test one's skills and be better before the ultimate showdown.
